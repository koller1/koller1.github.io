Case Router v1.0.0

Universal iOS Application

Case Router is built for government organizations using the Salesforce platform. It allows users to access case and contact record information as well as navigate to case locations using Google Maps.

Case Router is currently in use by the City of Elgin, IL.

Elgin 311 In Development

Universal iOS Application

Elgin 311 is a public facing mobile app for the City of Elgin, IL. It allows citizens to interface with the city's Salesforce community and view various city news feeds.

Currently, citizens can only view or create new 311 requests. After further Salesforce integration, citizens will be able to access the city's knowledge base and pay bills through the app.

Open Stadium In Development

Ruby on Rails App

Open Stadium is a sports social networking and news aggregation site built on the Ruby on Rails framework.

Users can explore the latest news on their favorite teams and engage in heated discussions with other fans.
